DEPARTMENT FACILITIES

Purpose of Rules, Policies and Quotas

Purpose of rules is to ensure smooth use of resources for all, and to not waste Research $$. We are a research department - most resources paid for out of research dollars - wasted resources mean less dollars for research and student support. Success of research is success for all department members. Good reputation will help in future job prospects for you. Purpose of quotas is to help us manage resources. We will always work with you if you need more resources to do your department related work. When in doubt, ASK!

Accounts

  1. Policies - no sharing, cheating, hacking, expiration.
  2. Choose a good password - Accounts with bad passwords are turned off.
  3. Quota - presently 2GB.
  4. Get an IC account (SINC site).
  5. Get a free email account (hotmail, gmail) and use it for personal business.
  6. Upon graduation we will provide an alumni address which will forward to account of your choosing.
