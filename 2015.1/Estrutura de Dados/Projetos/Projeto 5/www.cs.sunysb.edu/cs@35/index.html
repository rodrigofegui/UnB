May 10, 2005

Student Activities Center (SAC)

 

Thank you for joining us in our 35th Anniversary celebrations on May 10th. I invite you to renew your association with the Department of Computer Science at Stony Brook University.

You can review the schedule of events from the function, which included a gala banquet at the Charles B. Wang Center. John Swainson, President and CEO of Computer Associates, was the banquet speaker.

If you are an alumnus of the Computer Science Department at Stony Brook University, please register on our alumni website.

Arie E. Kaufman
Distinguished Professor and Chairman
