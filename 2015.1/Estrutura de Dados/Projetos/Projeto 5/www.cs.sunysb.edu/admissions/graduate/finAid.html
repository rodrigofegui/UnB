Financial Aid

To get an idea of the cost associated with graduate studies at Stony Brook we strongly recommend to check the Financial and Residential Information section in the Graduate School Bulletin.

Admitted Ph.D. students are typically offered financial support in the form of teaching or research assistantships, which include full tuition. Admission into this program is very competitive, though, and you must have the intension of completing this program. M.S. students are typically admitted with no financial aid. Many, however, find such aid on campus; typically by taking programming jobs for different administrative and academic units. The following page provides more information about financial help.
