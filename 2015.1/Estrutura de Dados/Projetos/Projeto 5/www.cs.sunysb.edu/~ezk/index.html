Erez Zadok

My name is Erez Zadok, and I am an Associate Professor at the Computer Science Department of Stony Brook University. I direct the Filesystems and Storage Lab at the CS department. My research interests include file systems and storage, operating systems, security, networking, and software engineering. You can send me email, and see my complete contact information and also my PGP key.
